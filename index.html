Résumé

I'm a Junior Software Developer & Devops Engineer based in Glasgow, UK. Currently I'm pursuing a PhD in Bioinformatics & Machine Learning supervised by Dr. John McClure, Dr. Simon Rogers and Prof. Darren Monckton. On software engineering side, my professional interests include continuous integration, software delivery pipelines, cloud integration and application containers. On the side of bioinformatics I'm interested in modeling evolutionary processes & data mining of large omics datasets.
